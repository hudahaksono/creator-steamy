Rewards and Incentives

Users engage with content on Streamy's Watch-to-Earn (W2E) platform to earn $STRM tokens, the ecosystem's native cryptocurrency, facilitating transactions and incentives.

  • Create-to-Earn (C2E)

    Streamy's Create-to-Earn (C2E) platform allows content creators to monetize their work on the blockchain, ensuring fair compensation and transparent transactions. With a user-friendly interface and vibrant community, creators can showcase their talents and engage with audiences globally.

  • Watch-to-Earn (W2E) Platform

    Streamy's Watch-to-Earn (W2E) platform rewards users for engaging with diverse multimedia content. Through active participation, users support creators, contribute to the community, and earn tokens directly into their wallets, fostering transparency and loyalty.
