Streamy AI: Unleashing the Power of AI for Your Ultimate Viewing Experience!

Streamy is an innovative platform that aims to revolutionize the content creation and monetization landscape by leveraging blockchain technology and Artificial intelligence (AI). We believe that AI should not control humans, but humans should control AI. and we can also utilize the blockchain network for transparent payment purposes.

The essence of Streamy lies in its commitment to bridging the gap between creators and their audiences, facilitating meaningful interactions and rewarding engagements. Through an intuitive and user-friendly interface, creators can easily upload, showcase, and monetize their content, while users can discover and engage with a diverse range of high-quality content across various genres and formats

Rewards and Incentives

Users engage with content on Streamy's Watch-to-Earn (W2E) platform to earn $STRM tokens, the ecosystem's native cryptocurrency, facilitating transactions and incentives.

  • Create-to-Earn (C2E)

    Streamy's Create-to-Earn (C2E) platform allows content creators to monetize their work on the blockchain, ensuring fair compensation and transparent transactions. With a user-friendly interface and vibrant community, creators can showcase their talents and engage with audiences globally.

  • Watch-to-Earn (W2E) Platform

    Streamy's Watch-to-Earn (W2E) platform rewards users for engaging with diverse multimedia content. Through active participation, users support creators, contribute to the community, and earn tokens directly into their wallets, fostering transparency and loyalty.
